History of TechCon

TechCon began in the early 2000s as a grassroots gathering of software developers in Silicon Valley. What started as a small meetup in a rented co-working space quickly grew into one of the world’s leading technology conferences.

Over the years, TechCon evolved into a hub for innovation, where pioneers in computing, networking, and digital design came together to share breakthroughs. It now hosts thousands of participants from across the world each year.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in technology. The conference serves as a bridge between industries, connecting engineers, researchers, and students who are shaping the future.

We believe in open knowledge, sustainable technology, and the power of community-driven progress. Each year, our events highlight initiatives that push humanity toward smarter and more ethical tech.

Past Speakers

Bill Gates

Co-founder of Microsoft, Bill Gates has been a long-time advocate for innovation and technology-driven education. At TechCon 2015, he delivered a keynote on global digital transformation and the ethics of artificial intelligence.

Sheryl Sandberg

As COO of Meta and founder of LeanIn.org, Sheryl Sandberg has championed leadership diversity in technology. Her 2017 talk at TechCon focused on empowering women in STEM and building inclusive workplaces.

Elon Musk

CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, Elon Musk has presented at multiple TechCon events. His sessions on renewable energy and interplanetary computing continue to inspire innovation across industries.
